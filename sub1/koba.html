Structure Refinement

The structure of the monomeric form of the potassium channel was submitted to KoBa-Min server for refinement via energy optimization (Rodrigues, Levitt and Chopra, 2012) where a dramatic increase in free energy was achievable, mainly by the addition of missing residues in the initial structure and refinement of bond lengths and andgles in the side chains of the aminoacid residues. As a remark it is to note that most of the missing residues where of hydrophillic nature, which are hard to cristalize properly in the contect of a membrane protein, since the majority of its composition is hydrophobic in nature.
